> By submitting your **first name** and **zip code** by email to the address above with the subject line `CHECKMATE`, you are providing a form of **electronic signature** in support of this petition. Your submission affirms that you are a real person who supports the principles of Operation Checkmate as set out in the Statement of Intent and the substantive provisions of this Act.

**Privacy:** Your name and zip code are collected solely to demonstrate citizen support for this proposal. Aggregated counts (by zip code, county, or state) may be shared publicly. **Your full identity will not be published** without your separate, explicit consent.

**Questions, edits, or feedback** on the draft are welcome at the same address — please use the same subject line `CHECKMATE` so that supporting messages and feedback are collected in one place.
