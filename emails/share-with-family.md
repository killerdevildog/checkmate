Hey [name],

I came across a bill called **Operation Checkmate** and I wanted to share it with you. It's a citizen-written proposal to make the people in government — judges, cops, prosecutors, politicians, inspectors, school officials — answer to the same law as everyone else.

Right now, when officials harm citizens — sometimes maliciously — there are stacked legal doctrines (judicial immunity, qualified immunity, prosecutorial immunity) that shield them from real accountability. This bill ends those doctrines, names them by case, and gives ordinary citizens — not the political class — the means to enforce accountability.

A few real-world things it addresses:

- A judge who approves taking a child without a real hearing — currently shielded; this bill makes them personally liable.
- A cop who pulls you over without a legitimate reason and goes fishing for a charge — reachable as "anarchy" (a government actor acting completely outside the law).
- A judge who threatens contempt to silence you when you're trying to assert a right — Official Intimidation, no immunity.
- A prosecutor who refuses to charge a friend or fellow official — obstruction of justice, at the same severity as the crime they refused to prosecute.
- Officials coordinating to ruin a citizen who won't go along — a felony called "cabalism."
- Out-of-state lobbyists with foreign money writing the laws that affect your life — a felony for both the lobbyist and the politician who acts on it.
