# Email — To a Representative or County Official

Template for sending **Operation Checkmate** to a representative, county commissioner, local official, or public-office candidate. Replace `[name]` and `[Your name]` before sending.

---

**Subject:** Please review Operation Checkmate

---

[Representative],

I thought about you and wanted your eyes on this.

I have been writing a bill called **Operation Checkmate**. Its purpose is to place every action of government under meaningful legal accountability.

Citizens are disciplined by criminal code. The military is disciplined by the **Uniform Code of Military Justice (UCMJ)**. But ordinary government power still lacks an equivalent discipline when officials misuse public authority against citizens. When a citizen violates the law, the criminal code reaches them. When a service member violates military duty, the UCMJ reaches them. But when a judge, prosecutor, officer, agency actor, regulator, elected official, or other government actor violates the rights of a citizen under color of authority, the system too often hides behind immunity doctrines, discretion, procedure, and institutional self-protection.

That is the gap Operation Checkmate is meant to close.

The principle behind Operation Checkmate is simple: **there are no kings in America**. No public official should have immunity for assaulting, battering, obstructing, or depriving the rights of a citizen under color of authority. Public power is a trust, not personal property, and rights should be enforceable against government actors when those actors abuse that power.

The missing piece in our system is a disciplined government. This bill is my attempt to define that discipline clearly in criminal code, so citizens have a real remedy when government authority is misused.

Please review it. If you support it, please follow the instructions at the bottom of the bill for signing on.
